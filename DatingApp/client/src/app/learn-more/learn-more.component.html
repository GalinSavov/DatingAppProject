About Dately

Connecting people in meaningful ways

Features

  • Match with people near you
  • Private messaging for you and your matches
  • Mutual like feature to prevent spam
  • Find connections based on shared interests

How It Works

Our app works by helping you find compatible people nearby. You can browse profiles, like others, and chat once there is a mutual match.

To start, simply create a profile, upload your picture, and fill out some basic information. Our matching algorithm does the rest!
